whats the assignment?

"Stadtkapelle Melk" (short: STKM), a fixed component of the Melk cultural associations, considered how to create another digital form of representing the association through established communication channels such as website, social media, print media. As part of the diploma thesis on the digitalization of city music band, a virtual tour will be created to provide a insight into the life of STKM. The artifacts will be made avaibale through a multimedia archive. Additionally, a digital storage system (multimedia archive) qill be created for artifacts and documents with backup security.

whats the big picture?

The central point of this diploma thesis is the digitalization of the local city band. Project "DigitalMuse" covers the representation and data organization for our partner "Stadtkapelle Melk". Data organization will be realized with the "Multimedia Archive". The representation will be implemented through a virtual tour about. Logo creation for recognition will be covered as a part of the project.

The assignment

The assignment was to deploy a wireframe and mockup about a sportwatch including two or more functions. Keyfocus has to be a simple realisation, which allows user to simple understand and use that device.

My realization

Step 1:

Create mindmap and define which requirements should be considered
  • unit system
  • powersupply work
  • maximum weight
  • Define targetgroup
  • Define which kind of sport should be considered
  • dimension of the actual watch
  • Buttons and functions placement
  • App for smartphone to transfer datas

Step 2:

Define the requirements
usage of metric system The metric system is the most known and common system. The watch will provide kilometer, m and european time format (e.g. 14:32).
powersupply Charging. To save weight and space, and even the environment. The user can simpley charge his sportwatch like his smartphone. The type of charger should be USB-c, its a global standard so I can assume that probably every customer has a USB-c charging cable, which means I can go a bit cheaper, because I dont have to provide a charging cable for the sportwatch.
